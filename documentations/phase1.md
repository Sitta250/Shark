# 🦈 Shark

## What it is

Shark is an AI-powered platform that turns raw business ideas into:
- a **viability assessment**
- a **market-informed strategy**
- a **clear execution plan**

It acts as a structured decision engine + workspace for founders.

---

## Why it exists

Most people:
- don’t know if their idea is worth pursuing
- waste time building before validating
- lack clear next steps

Shark solves this by:
- telling users **if an idea is worth it**
- showing **what matters and what doesn’t**
- giving **exact actions to move forward**

---

## Core concept

Shark is not a chatbot.

It is a **workflow-driven system**:

1. User inputs idea  
2. Shark analyzes (market, competition, risks)  
3. Shark outputs structured report  
4. User moves into execution workspace  
5. Shark guides ongoing progress  

---

## Core features (v1)

- Smart idea intake + adaptive questions  
- Opportunity scoring (go / cautious / avoid)  
- Market + customer analysis  
- Competitor breakdown  
- Business model recommendation  
- MVP / feature suggestions  
- Risk analysis  
- Compliance overview (country-aware)  
- 90-day execution roadmap  
- Exportable report  

---

## Workspace (retention layer)

Each idea becomes a **project workspace**:

- roadmap & milestones  
- tasks & checklists  
- assumption tracker  
- weekly check-ins  
- report refresh  
- notes & decisions
